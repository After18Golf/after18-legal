2. What After18 Does

After18 is a social golf scorekeeping and settlement-tracking app. It allows players to:

  • organise golf rounds
  • record hole-by-hole scores
  • calculate common golf side games
  • keep a record of who owes who after a round
  • mark whether those amounts have been settled outside the app.

After18 never holds, processes, transfers or facilitates money.

Responsible Play

Play socially. Play responsibly.

After18 exists to help friends keep score during social rounds of golf. It is a scorekeeping and settlement-tracking app only.

We do not run betting markets. We do not accept wagers. We do not hold money. We do not transfer money. We do not process payments. We do not take a commission or profit from any private arrangements between players.

Any friendly wagers are private agreements made entirely between the people playing.

Remember

  • Only participate if you are 18 years or older.
  • Only make friendly wagers with people you know and trust.
  • Never wager more than you can comfortably afford to lose.
  • If everyone is no longer comfortable with a wager, stop and enjoy the golf instead.
  • Keep golf fun, social and respectful.

You're always in control

Using After18 should never create pressure to continue betting. You can play without any wagers at all, stop using wagers at any time, or enjoy the app simply for scorekeeping and tracking results.
