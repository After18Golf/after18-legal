Account & Data Deletion

After18 · Last updated 19 July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your After18 account and the personal data associated with it, what is deleted, and what may be retained. After18 is operated by After18. If you have any questions, contact us at after18app@gmail.com.

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app (fastest): Open After18, go to Settings → Delete account, and confirm. Your account and personal data are removed straight away.

Option 2 — By email: If you can no longer access the app, email after18app@gmail.com from the email address registered to your account, with the subject line "Delete my account". We will verify the request and process it within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete or de-identify:

  • Your profile details: name, email address, phone number (if provided), handicap, Golf ID, PayID handle, and profile photo.
  • Your account login and authentication record.
  • Any feedback or bug reports you submitted, and chat messages you posted (these are removed or de-identified).

What may be kept, and for how long

  • Shared round history: rounds you played with other people may remain visible to those other players, with your entry shown as a removed or anonymised participant. This preserves the integrity of their own records.
  • Backups: encrypted backup copies may persist for up to 30 days after deletion before being permanently and securely erased.
  • Legal records: where we are required by law (for example, fraud prevention or dispute records), we retain only what the law requires, for only as long as required.

After18 does not process, hold, or transfer money. Any wagers recorded in the app are private, informal arrangements between friends and are settled directly between those users. Contact: After18, after18app@gmail.com.
